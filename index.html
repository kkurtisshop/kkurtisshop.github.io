About us

K Kurtis Ltd is a small boutique specialized in selling indo-western styled clothing and accessories for women at affordable budgets.

Our products are imported from India via air from online wholesalers

Our products are of high-quality cotton which are available in the market at affordable and unbeatable prices

Till date, the company has launched its own silk thread jewelleries and leather shoes (Jutti).

K Kurtis Ltd operates both online and in boutique at Vacoas from Monday to Saturday at 9 a.m to 4 p.m.

The company is well known virtually where it acquires mostly all its customers via Facebook and Instagram. Customers can thus, buy their products from the comfort of their home and have them delivered for free via post or at deliveries points

We have also started promoting our brand through media, started on April 2020 in Lifestyle magazine (which targets high profiled customers) and in 5Plus newspapers (which targets the general public).

K Kurtis Ltd focus on customer retention via loyalty cards and reward schemes which allows boosting of sales.
